CAFÉS

My favorite cafés in Madrid

100 Montaditos

Spanish fast-food chain available all over the city (and country - also available abroad!).

Address:

Plaza de las Cortes, 3.

What I like about it

Cheap, convenient, located all around the city. My favourite one is the one near the Prado museum.

Morfi Empanadas Argentinas

Great Argentinean empanadas in the heart of the city.

Address:

Calle Mayor, 44

What I like about it

Very nice meal deals, the empanadas are big and have a lot of filling.

Prep' La Crêpe

Salty and sweet crepes, as well as salads and smoothies. Large choice of drinks. Conveniently located in the city centre.

Address:

Calle de Preciados, 33.

What I like about it

Great crepes, nice staff, very cozy inside. Food portions are very big!
